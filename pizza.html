Pizza

Pizza from Mario's Pizza House

After completing the recipe your pizza should look as tasty as this. Hopefully yours will look like this after as well.

Just remember don't eat it all in one bite!

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Tomatoe Sauce
  • Dough
  • Banana Peppers

Steps

  1. Kneed the Dough
  2. Spread the Tomatoe Sauce
  3. Pour the Cheese
  4. Bake in Oven for 40 Minutes
  5. Enjoy!
