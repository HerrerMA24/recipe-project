Ham Sandwhich

Alondra's Sandwhich

This is how a beautiful sandhwich looks like. Although it's not as good as Alondra's

Just remember don't eat it all in one bite!

Ingredients

  • Ham
  • Mayo
  • Mustard
  • White Bread

Steps

  1. Toast the Bread
  2. Mix the Mayo and Mustard Together
  3. Spread it on bread
  4. Place ham on bread
  5. Enjoy!
